1. GOOGLE SEARCH CONSOLE (do this within 24h of deploying)
------------------------------------------------------------
This is how you tell Google the new site exists and tracks
how you're ranking.

  a. Go to https://search.google.com/search-console
  b. Add property: ehwlandscapes.com (domain property if
     you can edit DNS, otherwise URL-prefix property)
  c. Verify ownership (Google will give you a TXT record
     or HTML file to add)
  d. In the left sidebar -> Sitemaps -> add:
        sitemap.xml
     and submit.
  e. Use "URL inspection" on https://ehwlandscapes.com/
     and click "Request indexing".

Then come back to Search Console every couple of weeks and
check the Performance tab. After 4-6 weeks you'll see which
keywords are pulling impressions.

3. REVIEWS — THIS IS THE BIGGEST LEVER
------------------------------------------------------------
A Google Business Profile with 25+ Hayling Island reviews
beats one with 3, every time. This single thing will move
you up the Maps "3-pack" faster than anything else.

How to do it:

  a. In Google Business Profile, click "Share review form"
     and grab the short g.page link (looks like
     g.page/r/CXXXXXXXX/review).
  b. Save that link in your phone notes.
  c. After every finished job, text the customer:
        "Hi [name] — really enjoyed working on your garden.
         If you have 30 seconds, would you mind leaving us
         a quick Google review? [link]
         Thanks again, [Ellis/brother]"
  d. Aim for at least 1 new review per week for the first
     3 months. Quality + recency > quantity alone.

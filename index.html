FAQ

Common questions.

Do you offer regular garden maintenance on Hayling Island?

Yes. We offer weekly, fortnightly and monthly garden maintenance rounds across Hayling Island, covering mowing, weeding, hedge trimming and seasonal tidy-ups. Most gardens settle into a fortnightly rhythm through the growing season.

What gardening services do you provide?

Gardening, lawn mowing, weeding, fencing, hedge trimming, hardscaping and full garden maintenance. We work residential and small commercial gardens.

Do you do fencing work on Hayling Island?

Yes — feather-edge, panel, post-and-rail and gate installations. We replace storm-damaged fences and install new boundary fencing for Hayling Island, Havant and Emsworth properties.

Which areas do you cover?

Our core service area is Hayling Island. We also cover Havant, Emsworth, Waterlooville and the surrounding South Hampshire villages.

How do I get a quote?

Call or text us on 07469 237953 or send a short message via the contact form below. Quotes are free and we usually visit within a few days.

How much do you charge for garden work on Hayling Island?

Our rate is £22 per hour, per person. We always agree the scope before we start so there’s no surprises — quotes are free and we can usually visit within a few days.
